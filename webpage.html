About Me

Hey there, I'm Jayesh Verma, originally from Kota, Rajasthan. Currently, I'm a student at I.I.T. Roorkee, pursuing my degree in Chemical Sciences. Alongside my studies, I find immense joy in the world of design, which led me to join SDSLabs as a designer. I love letting my creativity flow and bringing ideas to life through design projects. Outside of academics and design, I indulge in my fascination with Indian languages, delving into their nuances and discovering the rich cultural heritage and history of my home state, Rajasthan.

My Projects

User Interface for Threads

This project, created for the Hexcode competition, aimed to enhance Instagram's Thread app with innovative features to boost its popularity. Our team's efforts earned us the Bronze medal. It was a rewarding experience that showcased our ability to craft impactful solutions and drive digital innovation.

Arthanomics Logo

In 2021, I had the privilege of designing the logo for Arthanomics, the annual Economics fest of Jai Hind College, Mumbai. It was a remarkable opportunity to contribute creatively to this prestigious event.

Board-Game Design

I designed a business and trading-themed board game for SDSLabs recruitment process. This fully playable game accommodates three to four players, offering an engaging experience that tests strategic thinking and decision-making skills.

Yaharaho.com

As a fun project, I designed the logo for Yaharaho.com, an imaginative venture providing student accommodation services. Inspired by creativity and functionality, the logo captures the essence of comfort and convenience for students' living needs.
